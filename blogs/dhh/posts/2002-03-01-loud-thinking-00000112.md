[Reboot](http://www.reboot.dk/)
ironclad, entrepreneur extraordinaire, and all-round nice guy
[Thomas Madsen-Mygdal](http://www.common.org/)
is set to make an appearence at the Copenhagen Business School. Wouldn't miss it for the world.

He'll be answering deep, philosophical questions, such as:
- What does it mean to be an entrepreneur?
- How do you find the spirit in your professional life?
- What's the benefits and demands of being an entrepreneur?

Available April 3rd, fluent in the Danish language, and resident of Copenhagen? Sign-up at
[seminar@doek.dk](mailto:seminar@doek.dk)
.

You'll need to fake being a student of CBS, but that shouldn't be too hard. Just say you're from HA.dat first year. I'll vouch for you.
