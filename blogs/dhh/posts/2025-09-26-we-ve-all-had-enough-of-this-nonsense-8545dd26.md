Every few years, the same sad contingent of Ruby malcontents tries to cancel me from Rails. At the peak of the woke era, back in 2022, they were actually successful in getting Ruby Central to
[uninvite me](https://world.hey.com/dhh/no-railsconf-faa7935e)
from doing the yearly keynote at RailsConf. But
[now RailsConf is dead](https://world.hey.com/dhh/the-last-railsconf-c6188593)
,
[Rails World is thriving](https://world.hey.com/dhh/thrice-charmed-at-rails-world-c4ed0006)
, and the cancellation nonsense is over.
Only I guess nobody told that same sad contingent! Because three days ago,
[they tried yet again](https://x.com/LundukeJournal/status/1970913813982515477)
, with the same trite grab bag of accusations: "he holds racist and transphobic views, as well as a number of other traits undesirable". And to add to the outrage theater, they named their little letter after a French resistance action
[fighting the Nazis](https://world.hey.com/dhh/calling-someone-a-nazi-is-a-permission-slip-for-violence-4bfbbb82)
during WWII. Subtle!
Except this time,
[nobody cared](https://x.com/dhh/status/1971240387265413324)
. In fact,
[quite the opposite](https://x.com/adamwathan/status/1971244361871696237)
.
[Thousands](https://x.com/awesomekling/status/1971287738268909576)
[of](https://x.com/pocarles/status/1970998193111634294)
[people](https://x.com/pberkenbosch/status/1971342547508437346)
[have](https://x.com/ikristoph/status/1970991013574607327)
[taken](https://x.com/r00t_fu/status/1971186764669911063)
[to](https://x.com/maietta/status/1971048091664122122)
[X](https://x.com/rafaeltorquat0/status/1971035270708633979)
and elsewhere to
[reject](https://x.com/markimbriaco/status/1971337909967167887)
[this](https://x.com/2disbetter/status/1970919839356289255)
[nonsense](https://x.com/dvassallo/status/1971278794875978112)
, and that's apparently making one of the organizers
[very sad](https://x.com/Grummz/status/1971214509177246096)
:

> *So far though, my experience has been that there are many more negative responses than positive. Maybe the Ruby community isn’t the place I thought it was, and MINASWAN was always a lie. That makes me sad. 😢*

I guess I would be sad too if I had named my group after THE GOOD GUYS and then it turned out that everyone thought I was THE BAD GUYS. But that's exactly what happened.
[The](https://x.com/ForrestPKnight/status/1971244955042742569)
[outpouring](https://x.com/dneighbors/status/1971072731824467975)
[of](https://x.com/repoles/status/1971160494011740498)
[support](https://x.com/Jonathan_Blow/status/1971254730958774679)
[from](https://x.com/taylorotwell/status/1971243592099438958)
[all](https://x.com/pell_0x/status/1971231016472936459)
[sides](https://x.com/justinhhorner/status/1971226111049912429)
[has](https://x.com/levelsio/status/1970804392774283473)
[been](https://x.com/pberkenbosch/status/1971342547508437346)
[overwhelming](https://x.com/DenLoginoff/status/1971312436235186526)
.
This is what it looks like when
[preference falsification](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Preference_falsification)
finally falls. When normal people are no longer afraid to
[say no to these people](https://world.hey.com/dhh/we-must-say-no-to-these-people-e0fb301c)
. Then it's revealed just how small and isolated these aggrieved individuals actually are.
Tobi from Shopify
[said it best](https://x.com/tobi/status/1970944464303923687)
:

> *It’s such a terrible mental tax on builders that divisive clowns just ride in and spew these bullshit terms that they clearly don’t understand themselves in bad faith. Ignore & keep building.*

That's exactly what we're going to do. We're going to reject and ignore these nut jobs. Then we're going to keep building.
