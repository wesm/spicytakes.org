We're hard at work finishing up
[Basecamp 1.0](http://www.37signals.com/basecamp/)
scheduled for launch in "January". With just five days of integrity left in that promise, there's a natural sense of urgency within the development team. But when both designer and programmer are natural customers of their own product, it's incredibly hard to resist gold-plating — the continuous stream of tweaks and small additions.
