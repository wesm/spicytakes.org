The first Building of Basecamp to leave the States is heading for its second home of Copenhagen. While that's a great thing in itself, it's even better that we're running in pairs with Reboot. The Copenhagen BoB is on the 9th on June.
[Reboot](http://reboot.dk)
starts on the 10th. So the city will already be filled with interesting people. You really have no excuse not to come.

We even have a spiffy
[new site for the workshop](http://www.37signals.com/workshop/)
in honor of taking it to Europe after much and heavy demand. The last workshop sold out in just one week. I wouldn't be putting off my registration, if I were you ;)
