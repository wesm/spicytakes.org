Joel Spolsky has been the biggest influence in my transition from caring about to loving code. I read every single article from
[Joel on Software](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/)
within days after discovering the site two years ago.

But reading Joel's carefully executed bursts of wisdom only left me longing for more. And on the
[Joel's Programmer's Bookshelf](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/navLinks/fog0000000262.html)
is where I found it. Freshly refined, it proposes nineteen steps to programming enlightenment.

I've taken nine of the steps so far. Each left me feeling noticebly smarter. I can't wait to stand at the top of the stairs.
