After four months of development, 37signals and I have completed and launched Basecamp 1.0. It's a hosted service for flexible project management composed of categorized weblogs sprinkled with contacts, milestones, and todo-list tracking. There's no fee for single-project setups, up to 10 active projects is $19/month, 25 is $39, and unlimited is $59. 37signals explains pricing and the offering in detail on
[BasecampHQ](http://www.basecamphq.com)
and answers questions on
[Everything Basecamp](http://everything.basecamphq.com/)
.
