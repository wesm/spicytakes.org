Trying to dress up unsubstantiated claims of bias as fact is hard enough as it is. With skillful application of humor and wit, it can some times be done. Slapping on
[the "really" sticker](http://php.weblogs.com/2003/07/07#a2697)
has never cut it, though:

PHP already has a larger user base, partly because it
**really**
is easier to use than Python, partly because Python's use of white-space is
**really**
too clever... [emphasis mine]

I mean, coooome on. I don't know whether to laugh or cry...
