We're tinkering with the
[Basecamp](http://www.basecamphq.com)
rates for the first time since the service launched soon-to-be a year ago. Doing so is always incredibly risky. No matter what you initially set your prices at, you've created a baseline and a set of expectations that are very hard to work against. In short, you've created a pricing reality and the increases needs to play within that logic.

So we deliberated long and hard over how we could make the adjustments as fair and evenly balanced as possible. The result is readable in
[Pricing and plan structure changes](http://everything.basecamphq.com/archives/000300.php)
at the Everything Basecamp site.

Now we're eagerly awaiting judgment from the constituency and how they will let their valets vote. Hopefully, we'll not alienate the bulk of them and hence be able to enter a brave new 2005 with a structure that'll allow us to reach above and beyond. It's going to be an awfully exciting year.
