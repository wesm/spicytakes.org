The job market for
[Ruby on Rails](http://www.rubyonrails.org/)
is heating up. I've been hooking up companies with developers left and right. And now, it seems, even the #rubyonrails IRC channel has turned into a potential job interview. We got quite a few firm owners hanging out and one of them just hired a developer off the channel. Thijs van der Vossen
[writes](http://www.vandervossen.net/2004/12/hiring_through_irc)
:

*This is probably the most nerdy thing I did this month: I just hired [Marten Veldthuis](http://www.standardbehaviour.com/) as a freelance Rails developer in the #rubyonrails IRC channel without ever meeting him in person*
.

That's pretty cool! Congratulations to both Marten and Thijs for hooking up like this.
