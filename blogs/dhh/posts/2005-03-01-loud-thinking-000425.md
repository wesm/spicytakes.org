Yahoo! Research Labs has a pretty cool new app out making markets out of buzz called
[Buzz Game](http://buzz.research.yahoo.com/bk/index.html)
. It uses the Yahoo search engine to identify hot topics and assigns a dollar value based on that. When you sign up, you get $10,000 to trade for an can invest as you see please.

Now the reason this is terribly interesting is of course that they have a
[Web Application Frameworks market](http://buzz.research.yahoo.com/bk/market/market.html?_mid=11024)
. And as I spotted this, I saw that
[Rails was already trading](http://buzz.research.yahoo.com/bk/market/market.html?_mid=11024&_ono=9)
at
*twice*
the value of number two (Flex) and more than four times the value of Java. Wow!

Puzzled as to what all this would mean, I asked recognized internet entrepreneur
[Thomas Madsen-Mygdal](http://www.commonme.org/)
. As the founder of internet conference
[Reboot](http://www.reboot.dk/)
, co-founder of wifi hotshot
[Organic](http://www.organicnetwork.net/)
, and a slew of other companies, I knew he would know. While Rails was trading at $22, he predicted:

*I see the core fundamentals in the Rails market taking it to 80 dollars six months. A strong buy recommendation.*

Holy moly! That's a four-fold increase prediction right there. Not one to distrust Thomas, I immediately invested all my starting capital of $10,000. Are you in yet?
