[DR1](http://www.dr.dk)
launched a new consumer watch-dog show called
[Kontant](www.dr.dk/kontant)
this evening. It's presented with the incredible and repeatedly excellence of the undoubtedly best Danish TV-journalist currently gracing our TV's: Mette Vibe Utzon. (Besides this new venture, she also runs
[Debatten](http://www.dr.dk/debatten)
— the best debate show I can remember ever seeing.)
