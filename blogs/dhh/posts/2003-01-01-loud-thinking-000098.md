[Michael Moore](http://www.michaelmoore.com/)
's fantastic
[Bowling For Columbine](http://www.bowlingforcolumbine.com/)
has been heavily promoted across Copenhagen on bus stops and commercial stands the past couple of weeks. Tonight it commanded a
[five-minute segment](http://www.dr.dk//nsapi.dr/newton/tva_indslag/tvindexview.asp?progdate=20030107&progtime=2100&viewflg=n)
on Danish TV-station
[DR1](http://www.dr.dk)
, which included the station's US-corespondent and a lector from a Danish University, who all debated the issues raised and the implications.

Despite (or rather thanks to) his
[Michief Marketing](http://mischiefmarketing.com/index_plain.html)
tactics, Michael Moore is being taken seriously and the issues he raises are getting attention.

Very cool.
