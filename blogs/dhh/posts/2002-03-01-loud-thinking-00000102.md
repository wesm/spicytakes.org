A new
[8-hour/day usage guideline](http://www.storagereview.com/)
has been issued for the IBM 120GXP series. What is this company thinking about? I used to hold IBM in high regard when it came to harddrives. That's definatly long gone.

I have a 30GB disk from the 75GXP series that's so likely to fail that IBM is being slapped with a
[class-action suit](http://www.tech-report.com/news_reply.x/3035/10/)
. The laptop harddrive that started all my
[Sony woes](http://www.loudthinking.com/archives/00000087.html)
is an IBM.

This company is not getting my data storage business for a decade.
