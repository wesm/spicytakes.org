How come
[everyone but me](http://www.nightmareproject.com/)
are exploring
[solar system colapses](http://www.nightmareproject.com/nmdsp.mv?16)
and getting acquinted with
[paralyzing chest monsters](http://www.nightmareproject.com/nmdsp.mv?216)
? I haven't had a dream I could remember for months, and I can count the number of nightmares I've ever had on a single hand.

Eight hours are spend every night sleeping and I've got nothing to show for it. Is it too much to ask for to get a few adventures of the mind just once in a while?
