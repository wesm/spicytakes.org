Rob Galbraith's pitting of
[two Macs against two PC's](http://www.robgalbraith.com/diginews/2003-01/2003_01_07_macpc.html)
on digital photography performance is interesting. Not because of the results, though. They repeat that PC's are faster and cheaper than what Apple can produce. Anyone with half an eye on Apple knows this.

It's interesting because they bubbles the whole issue of performance and makes it explicit for debate.
*What does performance mean and how much does it matter?*
These are fundamental questions that a lot of people are having a hard time evaluating the answers for.
