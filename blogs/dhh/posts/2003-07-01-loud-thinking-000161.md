Bubble Bobble is one of those golden classics that age gracefully and remains fun more than a decade since it was concieved. I've spend uncountable hours in the company of that green dragon and his bubbles.

So the joy would take no end when the combination of Frodo the C64 emulator and C64.com made it a
[mobile enjoyment](http://www.c64.com/detail.php?gameid=510)
. Or so I thought. The joy took a very disappointing end right as I discovered that the Nokia 3650 doesn't do diagonals.
