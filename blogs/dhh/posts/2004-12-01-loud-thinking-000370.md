[Rails](http://www.rubyonrails.org)
made the cover of Slashdot today with a story on
[RAD with Ruby](http://developers.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/12/06/1158251&tid=156&tid=1)
. Primarily a story on how KDevelop now has
[integrated support for Ruby](http://www.kdevelop.org/doc/technotes/rubyrad.html)
, but Amit managed to sneak in mentions of both Rails, the API for it, and my weblog. Thanks, Amit!

I'd recommend having a look at the
[10-minute video](http://media.nextangle.com/rails/rails_setup.mov)
, then reading Vincent's
[excellent tutorial](http://darkhost.mine.nu:81/~vince/rails/tutorial.html)
, and then getting jiggy with it using
[Gem Rails](http://www.rubyonrails.org/show/GemRails)
. Enjoy!

And do bring your questions to the FreeNode channel at #rubyonrails. We're a happy bunch of almost a hundred willing to answer loads of questions with almost endless patience.
