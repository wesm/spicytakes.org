If you're doing
[Painless Software Schedules](http://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000245.html)
on OSX, think about doing them in the
[$29.99 OmniOutliner](http://www.omnigroup.com/applications/omnioutliner/)
. It's incredibly easy and, unlike Spolsky's recommendation of
[$1,099 Microsoft Excel](http://www.microsoft.com/nz/mac/officex/officex_buy.asp)
, let's you manage subtasks effortlessly. This leds to more detailed schedules, which in turn leds to more precise and reusable scheduling.
