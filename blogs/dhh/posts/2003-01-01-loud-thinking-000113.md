Tracking progress on the iLife bundle is an excercise is subtlety. The global Apple site has started
[selling](http://store.apple.com/1-800-MY-APPLE/WebObjects/AppleStore?productLearnMore=M9063Z/A)
iLife today (before it was just pre-ordering), but still only vaguely talks about the downloadable updates (just that "Downloads will be available").

Meanwhile the local Danish Apple site is an hour an a half away from being factually incorrect as it
[promises](http://www.apple.com/dk/ilife/)
the bundle for download in "late January".

Apple could have handled this better.
