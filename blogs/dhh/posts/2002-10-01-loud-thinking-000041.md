[CommonMe.org](http://www.commonme.org)
is being hit by
[a Google query](http://www.google.com/search?q=caput%2Bthomas+madsen-mygdal)
on "caput+thomas madsen-mygdal". Mygdahl soundly
[theorizes](http://www.loudthinking.com/arc/000015.html#comments)
that saying "nicely put" to my
[Caput Crumbles piece](http://www.loudthinking.com/arc/000015.html#comments)
must have caused a stir with someone. To those who went looking, he offers a this highly reasonating piece of advice:

[T]he most important thing all of us can take from the last years are what we've learned. Not what the market, journalists, economy, etc. did wrong, but what we would have done differently with the power of the hindsight and knowledge that we've got today.

Stop looking out, start looking in.
