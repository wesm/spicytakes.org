I bought the
[fantastic Nokia 6630](http://www.loudthinking.com/arc/000363.html)
back in December under the assumption that Tiger would deliver on interoperability. How sadly disappointed I was when it didn't. But now, finally and out of the blue, Apple has updated
[iSync](http://www.apple.com/macosx/features/isync/)
to
[version 2.1](http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/isyncupdate21.html)
and alongside Address Book now
[supports](http://www.apple.com/macosx/features/isync/devices.html)
my lovely phone. Happy joy, joy.
