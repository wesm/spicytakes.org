With 438 downloads of the
[GEM version](http://www.rubyonrails.org/show/GemRails)
and 1581 downloads of the zip/tgz version,
[Rails](http://www.rubyonrails.org)
has climbed above and beyond two thousand downloads. This is roughly within the first month of release, so quite an achievement. In addition, the Rails packages take the second (AR), fifth (AP), and sixth (Rails) spot on the
[top 10 list over Ruby Gems](http://gems.rubyforge.org/stats.html)
.

Oh, and there's a new release coming shortly with a lot of funky goodies.
