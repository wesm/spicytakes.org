Something significant happened over Christmas (besides the consumption of unhealthy amounts of duck and other goodies). I completed the switch that
[began](http://www.loudthinking.com/arc/000010.html)
on August 22nd as of last year. My computer joys of both pleasure and business now belong to a
[12" Queen of Snow](http://www.apple.com/ibook)
.

It was the latter that held out for the months spanning September through December. Without a serious contender to either Netcaptor nor UltraEdit, it just didn't seem feasible. But with
[Chimera](http://www.mozilla.org/projects/chimera/)
clocking in at 0.6 and the
[Apple Project Builder](http://developer.apple.com/tools/projectbuilder/)
sporting
[PHP syntax highlighting](http://www.knaub.info/projekte/projekte.php)
there were only apologies left.

They be gone now.
