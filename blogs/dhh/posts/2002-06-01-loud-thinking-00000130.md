Has it really been three years? Apparently so,
[says CDNow](http://www.cdnow.com/cgi-bin/mserver/SID=1702529134/pagename=/RP/CDN/FIND/album.html/artistid=RED+HOT+CHILI+PEPPERS/itemid=658049)
, stating the release of Californication came about on June 8th, 1999. Back then, I was a year into my
[Bubble Dream](http://www.beyondvalueinvesting.com/articles/pyramidscheme.htm)
persuit, running a gaming website by the name of
[Quake3.dk](http://www.quake3.dk/)
. Wait, that's another story entirely.

Point:
[Red Hot Chili Peppers](http://www.redhotchilipeppers.com/)
is but three weeks away from releasing their 9th album (though I only cared since Californication) and the first single is already out. The video has been spinning relentlessly on MTV for good reasons: Anthony Kiedis' voice is still engrossing,
[the lyrics](http://www.azlyrics.com/lyrics/redhotchilipeppers/bytheway.html)
still sends my thoughts running, and the riff still brings me to the edge of what I can take while enjoying it.

Needless to say, I'm one Red Hot Fan having Red Hot Anticipations for July 9th. Should it contain just half the amount (3) of killer tracks as Californication (6), RHCP will easily retain my fandom and I humbly sit tight for another three years.

*(Now I'm really feeling bad about not being in [Roskilde](http://www.roskilde-festival.dk/) on [Friday the 28th of this month at 2200 hours](http://www.roskilde-festival.dk/bands/tid_alle_2002.php4))*
