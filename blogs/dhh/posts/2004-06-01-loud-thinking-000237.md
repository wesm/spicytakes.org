This is the fourth release of
[Active Record](http://activerecord.rubyonrails.org/)
in as many days. So while I perhaps didn't follow the first part of "release early, release often", I am surely following the latter. The same was true for
[Instiki](http://www.instiki.org)
, which underwent 9 releases in just two months. Open source has a habit of bringing that kind of rapid improvement to products.
