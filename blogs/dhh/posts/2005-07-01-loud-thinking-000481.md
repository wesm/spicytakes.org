Considering that Ruby on Rails is powering one of the premier destinations for podcasting with
[Odeo](http://www.odeo.com)
, it couldn't stand long that we didn't have our own show.
[Scott Barron](http://scott.elitists.net/)
is the slick voice at the mic and I'm the first interviewee to appear:
[Ruby on Rails Podcast](http://podcast.rubyonrails.org/)
.

Yes, the sound could be a whole lot better. And it will be for the next edition. Scott is just coming to terms with the whole thing, recording everything off the iBook built-in mic. We'll be sure to equip him with some proper gear for next week.

The goal is to make this show a weekly occurrence. Scott already has a bunch of interview subjects lined up. Subscribe to
[the feed](http://podcast.rubyonrails.org/podcast.xml)
and we'll even deliver.
