Mauricio Fern�ndez has been working on
[Ruby Production Archive](http://rpa-base.rubyforge.org/wiki/wiki.cgi?RpaBase)
as an alternative packaging scheme to
[RubyGems](http://rubygems.rubyforge.org/wiki/wiki.pl?RubyGems)
. I'm still more of a GEM-guy myself, but Mauricio does deserve recognition for packaging both
[Instiki](http://www.instiki.org)
and
[Rails](http://www.rubyonrails.org)
for RPA. Especially the latter is quite a task (which is why I haven't done a GEM for it myself, yet).

On top of that, Mauricio has taken a cue from the success of the 10 minute video for Rails and done a
[RPA video for installing Rails](http://rpa-base.rubyforge.org/wiki/wiki.cgi?Rails_Usage)
. It's pretty cool. He taped the whole thing through his prompt using Links to access the web-server. Very retro.
