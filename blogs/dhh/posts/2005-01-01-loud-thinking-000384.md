I've been a test-driven developer for quite a while, but I haven't really gotten into the religious test-
*first*
branch yet. Usually, I'm too impatient. I have a good idea and want to execute on it right away. Then, when I see it in code, I'll come back to do the tests.

I have done short spurts of test-first — some tasks just seem to invite it — but not as a regular approach over a longer and sustained period of time. I want to give that a try. For a month. Where I can't right any code until I've seen a test fail.

It's
[one](http://www.43things.com/things/view/7384)
of
[my 43 things](http://www.43things.com/people/view/David)
.
