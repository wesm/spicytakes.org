It's been far too long since the logo commissioned by the community was completed by John Hicks. But I think it was worth the wait to hold it back until the long-awaited relaunch of the Ruby on Rails website could happen. And today,
[it did](http://www.rubyonrails.com)
!

The new website brings together all many web properties revolving around Rails. There's the brand new Hieraki-powered
[Manuals](http://manuals.rubyonrails.com)
area, there's the
[Trac](http://dev.rubyonrails.com)
for patches and tickets, and of course we still have Instiki running the
[community](http://wiki.rubyonrails.com)
area.

In short, it's all there. And we're mighty proud to finally be able to share it with the world!
