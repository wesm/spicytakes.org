The
[O'Reilly Open Source Convention](http://conferences.oreillynet.com/os2005/)
is happening on August 1st until 5th and I just got the official confirmation that both of my proposals have been accepted. I'll be doing a tutorial and a session talk:

I'm really honored to see both proposals being picked up. Especially considering O'Reilly had to

[at least one book about Rails](http://www.pragmaticprogrammer.com/titles/rails/index.html)
out too.
