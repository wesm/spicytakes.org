I recently talked withJulien Verlaguet. Julien is the founder ofSkipLabs, a company building infrastructure for reactive applications. Before SkipLabs, Julien spent 9 years at Facebook, where he was a tech lead forHackandSkiplang.


In this interview, we discuss SkipLabs's foundational components:SKDB, SkipStorage, and Skiplang. SKDB is built as a reactive database that features performant materialized views, diffing between databases, and streaming via ephemeral tables. SkipStorage and Skiplang serve as building blocks for SKDB.


By the time you hear this, SkipLabs will have launched, so I invite you to checkskiplabs.ioandskdb.iofor more details. In the meantime, please enjoy the following conversation with Julien.
