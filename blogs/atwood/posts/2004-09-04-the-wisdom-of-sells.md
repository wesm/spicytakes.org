Chris Sells is one of those rare developers who is so talented at both coding and communicating that **everything he writes is worth reading**. How I wish this was more common! If you haven’t already, read through [his archived Spout posts](https://web.archive.org/web/20041010193409/http://www.sellsbrothers.com/spout/default.aspx?content=archive.htm); there’s some really great stuff in those two dozen entries. Don’t forget [the latest Spout content](https://web.archive.org/web/20041015080920/http://www.sellsbrothers.com/spout/), either.


And, of course, you’ll want to bookmark [his blog](http://www.sellsbrothers.com/).
