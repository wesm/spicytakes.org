After two weeks of non-stop tweaking, I think my [MAME Cocktail arcade](https://blog.codinghorror.com/mame-cocktail-arcade/) is finally complete. I created a [MAME cocktail project page](https://web.archive.org/web/20060217065654/http://www.codinghorror.com/mamecocktail/) documenting everything I’ve done so far with lots of pictures and links to the products I used, and the rationales behind the choices I made.


Most of the comments there apply to [MAME](http://www.mame.net/) cabinets of all types, so if you have any interest in classic arcade gaming at all (or perhaps, like my coworkers, you’re obsessed with [Golden Tee](https://web.archive.org/web/20050206193149/http://www.itsgames.com/Products/GT2005/DEFAULT.htm)), it’s worth reading.
