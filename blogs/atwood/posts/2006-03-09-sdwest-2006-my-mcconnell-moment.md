I’ll be attending [SDWest 2006](http://www.sdexpo.com/) all next week (March 13 - 17) in Santa Clara, California.


If you’re attending, let’s meet up somewhere! I’ve met a handful of people in person at past conferences:

- [Sara Ford](https://web.archive.org/web/20060314170136/http://blogs.msdn.com/saraford/default.aspx)
- [Steve Maine](https://web.archive.org/web/20060417183821/http://hyperthink.net/blog/)
- [Walt Rischler](https://web.archive.org/web/20060327134343/http://waltritscher.com/blog/ramblings/)


The highlight of SDWest, however, will hopefully be meeting [Steve McConnell](http://www.stevemcconnell.com/), who is giving the keynote on Monday.


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/05/image-228.png)


McConnell was **my first coding hero**.


I discovered his book [Code Complete](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0735619670) in 1994 and it was a revelation. Programming is such a solitary pursuit, and particularly so in those pre-internet days. In Code Complete, I found an amazingly passionate, incredibly literate, and consummately professional programmer to learn from. **Code Complete convinced me that writing software is what I want to do for the rest of my life**. The book – now upgraded to [version 2.0](https://blog.codinghorror.com/code-complete-2-the-revenge/) – has been my bible ever since.


**Update**: Steve was very gracious, talking at some length with me both before and after his keynote. He even signed my copy of Code Complete 2. As he said, “I guess I should sign on a page with a Coding Horror:”


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/03/code-complete-2-signed-copy-closeup.png)

*"To Jeff Atwood with every good wish. Hope my signature isn't too horrifying!" – Steve McConnell, Santa Clara CA, March 13, 2006*
