Wil Shipley, the entity behind [Delicious Library](http://arstechnica.com/reviews/apps/delicious-library.ars), has a hilarious (and informative) talk on why he develops software for the Mac – and also netted $54,000 from Delicious Library on the first day with zero advertising.


[How to Succeed Writing Mac Software](https://web.archive.org/web/20051109095022/http://wilshipley.com/blog/WWDC_Student_Talk.pdf) (176kb PDF)


One of the funniest and most oddly inspiring things I’ve read in a long time. Highly recommended.
