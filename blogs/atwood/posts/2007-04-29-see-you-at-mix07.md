I'm heading off to [MIX07](https://web.archive.org/web/20070714071038/http://www.visitmix.com/) today.


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/03/image-250.png)


MIX is by far my favorite Microsoft conference, because it "mixes" in a liberal dose of [traditionally non-Microsoft folks](https://web.archive.org/web/20070723155209/https://content.visitmix.com/(S(lxq41qfrt22mbr45zysy5brp))/public/sessions.aspx) for a **broader range of perspectives**. It's probably the only Microsoft conference I'll be attending this year.


Vertigo is also presenting something special at MIX: our new [Family.Show WPF reference app](https://web.archive.org/web/20070502024025/http://www.vertigo.com/familyshow.aspx).


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/03/image-249.png)


If you're attending MIX this year and you're interested in meeting up, shoot me an email. I'll definitely bring lots of stickers.


I also set up a Coding Horror Twitter stream for MIX related activities, and I'll try to keep it updated throughout the conference, barring any [performance meltdowns](https://blog.codinghorror.com/twitter-service-vs-platform/) – for example, right now Twitter's static asset server appears to be down, so no images or stylesheets appear.
