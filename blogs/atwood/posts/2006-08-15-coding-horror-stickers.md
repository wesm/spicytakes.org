As I alluded to in the [T-Shirt post](https://blog.codinghorror.com/own-a-coding-horror/), Coding Horror stickers have arrived:


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/04/image-752.png)


These are custom, two color die-cut vinyl stickers based on the high resolution vector art so graciously provided by our kind benefactor, [Steve McConnell](http://www.stevemcconnell.com/). To give you an idea of scale, the coin in the picture is a nickel. The dimensions of the sticker are 3.55" h × 3" w.


As a thank you to my readers, **anyone who emails me from now until midnight PDT Wednesday, August 16th gets a free Coding Horror sticker.** Please make the title of the email "free sticker", and don’t forget to provide your full postal address. You must live in the US or Canada.*


Update: we’ve reached 350 stickers; the free sticker offer is now closed. I totally [blame Mike Gunderloy](https://web.archive.org/web/20071011233003/http://www.larkware.com/dg6/TheDailyGrind948.aspx). I’ll try to get all the stickers out by Monday. I think you’ll really like them!


*Sorry, international readers. Dealing with postage rate scales makes my tiny American brain hurt.
