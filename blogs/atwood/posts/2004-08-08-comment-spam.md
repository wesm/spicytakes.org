I like the way anyone can leave a comment regarding my posts – the only good discussion is a two-way discussion – but the comment spam is getting way out of hand (viagra, gambling, penis enlargement, ad nauseam). Thank goodness, then, for [MT-blacklist](http://www.jayallen.org/projects/mt-blacklist/), a simple anti-spam plugin for Movable Type.


As promised, very easy to install, and based on a few of my tests, it definitely works! If you’re interested – and if you are using Movable Type 2.x, you should be – check out [the documentation](http://www.jayallen.org/projects/mt-blacklist/latest/index) for details.


p.s. SUCK IT SPAMMERS!
