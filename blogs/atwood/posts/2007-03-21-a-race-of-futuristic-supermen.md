I’ve seen a lot of painfully bad IT web comics in my day, but I’m happy to say that Bug Bash, by Hans Bjordahl, is not one of them.


![How Good Software Goes Bad, Part I](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/uploads/2007/03/6a0120a85dcdae970b0120a86d7b40970b-pi.png)


This particular strip is one of my favorites because it hits so close to home. Software developers truly believe, in their heart of hearts, that [they are typical users](https://blog.codinghorror.com/the-rise-and-fall-of-homo-logicus/). Be sure to check out the sequel strip, [How Good Software Goes Bad, Part 2](https://web.archive.org/web/20070528041915/http://www.bugbash.net/comic/44.html), and while you’re at it, the rest of [the bug bash archives](https://web.archive.org/web/20070520081438/http://www.bugbash.net/archives/comic).
