The year 2005 marks the [20th anniversary of Windows 1.0](https://web.archive.org/web/20060307205353/http://internet.ls-la.net/ms-evolution/windows-1.01/):


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/05/image-36.png)


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/05/image-35.png)


The first version of Windows I actually used was **Windows 3.0**. Coming from an Amiga background, I was unimpressed. It wasn’t until [Windows 3.1](https://web.archive.org/web/20060209043225/http://internet.ls-la.net/ms-evolution/windows-3.1/) and [Windows For Workgroups 3.11](https://web.archive.org/web/20050208064417/http://internet.ls-la.net/ms-evolution/windows-3.11/) that I actually started to believe Microsoft had something worth using.
