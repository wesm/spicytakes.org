Why does the sporkfe fascinate me so?


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/04/image-764.png)


It’s a spoon. It’s a fork. It’s a knife. Some call it a [splade](http://www.everything2.com/index.pl?node_id=1463951) – sold commercially in Australia for the last 50 years under the [Splayds](https://www.splayd.com.au/) brand name – but I prefer *sporkfe*. Really, when was the last time you ate your food with a *blade?*


I have no idea why I bought these. But at least I saved myself from the indignity of owning [The Mother of All Swiss Army Knives](https://web.archive.org/web/20061006211132/http://blogs.vertigosoftware.com/jatwood/archive/2006/08/24/The_Mother_of_All_Swiss_Army_Knives.aspx).
