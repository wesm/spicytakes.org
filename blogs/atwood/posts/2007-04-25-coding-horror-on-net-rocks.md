It was my great honor to participate in this week’s [episode of ](https://www.dotnetrocks.com/details/232)[.NET Rocks!](https://www.dotnetrocks.com/details/232)


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/09/dot-net-rocks-podcast.jpg)


[.NET Rocks!](https://www.dotnetrocks.com) is a long running internet radio talk show for software developers that goes all the way back to 2002. I’ve listened to their shows off and on for years. They’ve interviewed some very notable software developers along the way [including Steve McConnell](https://www.dotnetrocks.com/details/215), and many other people far more interesting than myself. One of the earliest interviews (#11, to be precise) was [with our Vertigo Software CEO, Scott Stanfield](https://www.dotnetrocks.com/details/11).


My interview is 64 minutes long, and explores some common themes I’ve covered here in my blog.
