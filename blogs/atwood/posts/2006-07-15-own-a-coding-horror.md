A few people recently pointed out that [my personal branding](https://blog.codinghorror.com/your-personal-brand/) isn’t everything that it could be. Joseph Cooney even [took matters into his own hands](https://web.archive.org/web/20061211142713/http://jcooney.net/archive/2005/04/21/512.aspx).


Well, I contacted the big man himself, [Steve McConnell](https://www.stevemcconnell.com/), and he graciously provided me a high resolution vector file of the original Coding Horror logo used in [Code Complete](https://www.amazon.com/Code-Complete-Practical-Handbook-Construction/dp/0735619670) (see it [on the page](https://blog.codinghorror.com/about-me/)), along with permission to sell items based on that design.
