A few years ago, I did a bit of research on computer desks and decided on this AnthroCart model:


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/05/image-243.png)


And it’s a fantastic computer desk. But its Wife Acceptance Factor ([WAF](https://web.archive.org/web/20060420091556/http://www.boston.com/business/technology/articles/2004/06/28/waf_wife_acceptance_factor/)) is abysmal for a few reasons:

- It’s absolutely enormous
- It’s black
- It’s design is better suited to an office than a home


Plus, we just bought a small home in the Bay Area,* and we need to optimize for space. Looks like I’ll be selling the Anthrocart.


Which means I’m looking for a smaller desk now.


I had to buy an additional desk when I moved to California, prior to all our stuff arriving. Since we have easy access to IKEA, I went with the IKEA Jerker desk.


![](https://blog.codinghorror.com/content/images/2025/05/image-242.png)


You can see the edge of the Jerker in the bottom-most photo of [Phil’s dog Twiggy](http://haacked.com/archive/2005/03/23/2432.aspx). There’s something about the Jerker that inspires people: Behold! The shrine to [the IKEA Jerker desk!](http://adam.pra.to/content/jerker/)


Although I really do like the Jerker – and the name alone is worth the 99 bucks – I’m still researching my options. **What is your favorite computer desk, and why?**
