Back in June, I mentioned that [my favorite visual differencing tool](https://blog.codinghorror.com/visual-diff-tools/) was Araxis Merge. A co-worker recently recommended that I try out [Beyond Compare](http://www.scootersoftware.com/home.php), so I downloaded the 30-day trial and spent an hour playing with it. It’s definitely comparable to Araxis Merge. And in a lot of ways, it’s better. But the real kicker is this:

- [Araxis Merge single license](http://www.araxis.com/merge/purchasing.html): **$129**. 5 or more, **$83** each
- [Beyond Compare single license](http://www.scootersoftware.com/shop.php): **$30**. 5 or more, **$17** each


Fatality.


It’s better than Araxis Merge and it’s one-third the price! Beyond Compare is the kind of finely crafted software that inspires you to register it. Give the 30-day trial version a shot and I bet you’ll feel the same way.


BC not only matches Merge in terms of visual differencing features, but **it also doubles as a kick ass project deployment tool**. I like [Unleashit](http://www.eworldui.net/unleashit/), but sometimes you get what you pay for.


Because of the deep multiple license discount, we decided to buy five licenses for work. That’s still $44 less than one copy of Araxis Merge! If you also decide to register BC, don’t forget to download the [filetype specific comparison plugins](http://www.scootersoftware.com/download.php?c=v2plugins), too.
