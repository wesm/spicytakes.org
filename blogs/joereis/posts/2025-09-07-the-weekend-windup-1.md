*Welcome to the Weekend Windup. I’m wound up because AI keeps getting the image wrong…*


Welcome to the Weekend Windup, a new edition to my Substack. Each weekend (Saturday and Sunday will vary depending on travel and such), I’ll feature some cool things I’ve read, and some awesome events I’m either participating in, or very stoked on.


“But Joe, how is this different from your other Substack posts?”Here goes my latest iteration on a weekend newsletter. In the past, this often manifested itself as a single (and long) weekend newsletter. Recently, a lot more than usual is on my mind. More observations and more opportunities = an overactive mind far more overactive.


Weekend newsletters are hard. You have things to do, and your attention is very limited. I want to respect that. Hence, I’m keeping the short parts and long reads for the weekend, so you can hopefully enjoy your time, and possibly read my brief rant, and some articles and events I share here.


In the next weekend windup, this space will be occupied by a brief rant on whatever’s on my mind.


For now, have a fun weekend,


Joe
