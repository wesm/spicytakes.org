Hi! It’s Valentine’s Day ❤️ weekend. Also, I’m in the middle of finishing sections of mydraft chapteron alt-relational data modeling. So I’ll keep this short.Something that’s been on my mind for a while is the disconnect between “Product” and those responsible for delivering on the product vision, usually engineering and data/ML teams. Different expectations, vocabulary, and timelines, among other ailments. It’s confusing and frustrating. What if we could unify these two universes?


In this conversation with my two friends, Anne-Claire Baschet and Yoann Benoit (who also have an upcoming book onNerd Herd), we unpack these questions and more. Also, check out their awesome article,AI & Product: Two Development Cycles to Unify. Enjoy!


This video (or audio) is also available on your podcast platform of choice likeSpotify,Apple Podcasts, orYouTube.


Have a wonderful weekend,
