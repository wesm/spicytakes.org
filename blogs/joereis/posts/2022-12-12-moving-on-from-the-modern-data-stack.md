Sarah Catanzaro is a general partner at Amplify Partners, which invested in dbt, Motherduck, and many other high-profile data and technology companies. She joined the Monday Morning Data Chat to discuss what should happen after the Modern Data Stack, how she looks at potential investments, the advances in AI/ML, and next-generation databases.


There’s a lot packed in this one-hour episode, so I hope you enjoy it!
