Needless to say, it involves a ton of hard work that you’ll probably not want to do, and an insane amount of luck that you’ll falsely attribute to your not-so-hard work (I joke, but not really). Either way, if you want to get a very rare glimpse into the technical book writing schtick, here you go. Enjoy.
