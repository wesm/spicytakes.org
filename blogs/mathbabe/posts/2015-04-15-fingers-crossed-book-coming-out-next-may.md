As it turns out, it takes a while to write a book, and then another few months to publish it.


I’m very excited today to tentatively announce that my book, which is tentatively entitled [*Weapons of Math Destruction: How Big Data Increases Inequality and Threatens Democracy*](https://mathbabe.org/2014/01/13/im-writing-a-book-called-weapons-of-math-destruction/), will be published in May 2016, in time to appear on summer reading lists and well before the election.


Fuck yeah! I’m so excited.
