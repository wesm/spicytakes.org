Yesterday *[Slate](http://www.slate.com/)* published a piece I wrote for them entitled [The Ethical Data Scientist](http://www.slate.com/articles/technology/future_tense/2016/02/how_to_bring_better_ethics_to_data_science.html). Take a look and tell me what you think, I enjoyed writing it.


One thing I call for in the essay is the teaching of ethics to aspiring data scientists, and yesterday some very cool professors from the Berkeley School of Information wrote to me and told me about their two classes on data science and ethics, [one for undergrads](http://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/courses/i88a) and [the other for graduate students](http://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/courses/datasci231). I seriously wish I could enroll in them!


Please tell me of other efforts in this direction if you know of them.
