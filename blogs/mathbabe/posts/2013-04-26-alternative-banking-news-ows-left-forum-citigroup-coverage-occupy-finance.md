The [Alternative Banking group of OWS](http://alternativebanking.nycga.net/) is still meeting every Sunday at Columbia from 3-5pm to discuss financial reform. We also often have pre-meeting talks open to the public. The normal meeting is open to the public as well, but it can get pretty wonky. This week’s pre-meeting talk is an update on Greece.


Please join us! Details are [here](http://alternativebanking.nycga.net/come-to-alternative-banking-meetings/), on our [blog](http://alternativebanking.nycga.net/). You can also sign up for the normal meeting and pre-meeting through Meetup [here](http://www.meetup.com/Alternative-Banking-Group-Occupy-Wall-Street-of-NYC/).


**Left Forum**


We just found out our proposal for a panel at [the Left Forum](http://www.leftforum.org/) got accepted ([here we are](http://www.leftforum.org/content/occupy-wall-street-special-meeting-alternative-banking-group-breaking-megabanks) on their website). In case you don’t know much about the Left Forum, it’s a conference taking place on June 7-9th at Pace University in New York, which brings together a huge number of lefties and progressives and activists together to talk about their work. It used to be called the [Socialist Scholars Conference](http://www.keywiki.org/index.php/Socialist_Scholars_Conference). Noam Chomsky and Cornel West are scheduled to be there this year.


Our plan for the panel is to have a “regular meeting” at the conference. So anyone in our group can come (I think they have to register first), which means anyone at all, since our group is open to the public. Our probably topic will be “Breaking up the megabanks” or something along those lines.


I’ll update when we find out when we’re scheduled to go on. [Here’s](http://www.leftforum.org/panels/approved/2013) a full list of approved panel topics.
