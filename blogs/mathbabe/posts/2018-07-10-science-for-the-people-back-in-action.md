The good folks at [Science For The People](https://scienceforthepeople.org/) are hosting an event to celebrate their first publication in a long while (see more about the history of [SftP here](https://scienceforthepeople.org/index.php/about-sftp/)):


Celebrate the return of [Science for the People](http://caveat.nyc/event/science-for-the-people-geoengineering-launch-documentary-premiere/)! Join us for the premiere viewing of the mini-documentary about SftP’s revitalization and readings from our first publication since 1989: a collection of essays about the science and politics of geoengineering.


Doors: 6:30pm

Showtime: 7:00pm

Address: Caveat NYC, 21A Clinton Street, New York NY 10002

Tickets: [$15 adv / $20 door](http://caveat.nyc/event/science-for-the-people-geoengineering-launch-documentary-premiere/)


I hope I see you there!
