This Sunday there’ll be a huge march to raise awareness about climate change. It’s called [the Climate Convergence](http://globalclimateconvergence.org/), and the [Alternative Banking group](http://altbanking.net/) is going to be there.


If you want to join us, come to 79th and Central Park West at 11am, in front of the Natural History Museum. We will have signs and a banner. See you there, I hope!
