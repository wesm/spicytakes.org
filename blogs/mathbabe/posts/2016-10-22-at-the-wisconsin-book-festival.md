I arrived in Madison last night and had a ridiculously fantastic meal at [Forequarter in Madison](http://www.forequartermadison.com/#home) thanks to my friends Shamus and Jonny of the [Underground Food Collective](http://www.undergroundfoodcollective.org/).


Delicious!


I’m here to give a talk at the [Wisconsin Book Festival](http://www.wisconsinbookfestival.org/), which will take place [today at noon](http://www.wisconsinbookfestival.org/events/weapons-math-destruction), and I’m excited to have my buddy [Jordan Ellenberg](http://www.math.wisc.edu/~ellenber/) introduce me at my talk.


I’ll also stop by beforehand at [WORT](https://www.wortfm.org/) for a conversation with Patty Peltekos on her show called *[A Public Affair](http://www.wortfm.org/news-talk/talk/public-affair/)*, as well as afterwards at the local NPR station, [WPR](http://www.wpr.org/), for a show called *[To The Best of Our Knowledge](http://www.ttbook.org/)*. These might be recorded, I don’t know when they’re airing.


What a city! Very welcoming and fun. I should visit more often.
