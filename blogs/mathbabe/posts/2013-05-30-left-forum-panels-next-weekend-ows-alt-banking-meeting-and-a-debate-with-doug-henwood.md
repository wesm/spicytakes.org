Next weekend at Pace University in New York City I’ll be taking part in two panels at the [Left Forum](http://www.leftforum.org/), a yearly conference of progressives that everybody who’s anybody seems to know about, although this will be my first year there. For example [Noam Chomsky is coming](http://www.leftforum.org/content/noam-chomsky-2013-left-forum-plenary-address-part-1) this year.


First, from noon til 1:40 on Saturday June 8th, I’ll be debating how to shrink the financial sector with Doug Henwood, author of [Wall Street: how it works and for whom](http://www.amazon.com/books/dp/0860916707). The panel will be moderated by my buddy [Suresh Naidu](http://new.sipa.columbia.edu/faculty/suresh-naidu), an occupier [profiled in the Huffington Post](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/11/11/occupy-wall-street-zuccotti-columbia-economics-professor_n_1082766.html). The announcement for this panel is [here](http://www.leftforum.org/content/shrinking-financial-capital-why-and-how) and includes room information.


Second, from 3:40 til 5:20, also on Saturday June 8th, I’ll be facilitating a meeting of the [Alternative Banking group of OWS](http://alternativebanking.nycga.net/), which will be loads of fun. The idea is to explain to the panel audience how we roll in Alt Banking, to have a discussion about breaking up the banks, and to get the audience to participate as well. We expect them to enjoy getting on stack. The announcement for this panel is [here](http://www.leftforum.org/content/occupy-wall-street-special-meeting-alternative-banking-group-breaking-megabanks), please come!


Registration for the Left Forum is still open and is affordable. Go [here](http://www.leftforum.org/civicrm/event/info?reset=1&id=677) to register, and see you next weekend!
