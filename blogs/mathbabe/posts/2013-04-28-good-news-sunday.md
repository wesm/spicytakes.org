Sunday mornings tend to provoke me to write the most whimsical posts of the week. I’ve usually gotten enough sleep for the first time in seven days, unless I’m hung over from Saturday late-night karaoke (but I usually like to do that on Fridays), and I can actually remember some of my dreams.


Especially on a glorious sunny Spring morning like today, I can’t abide any bad news or ranting. So today *it’s only gonna be good news*. Here goes.


**1) Chocolate Fondue**


Did you know that you can buy [chocolate fondue machines for like $9.00](http://www.amazon.com/Nostalgia-Electrics-CFF300-Chocolate-Fountain/dp/B008OH4TX0/ref=sr_1_7?ie=UTF8&qid=1367148284&sr=8-7&keywords=chocolate+fondue)? I found this out because at some point I realized my kids hadn’t had chocolate fondue in ages (trust me, it was *all about the kids*), and I wanted to find a place in New York City where we could go eat fondue, but the only places offering it were like $300 meals for a family of five. So I went on Amazon instead, and paid for the whole shebang for under $30, including [the little sticks](http://www.amazon.com/Fox-Run-Brands-Bamboo-Skewers/dp/B000UJ0Z10/ref=pd_sim_k_2), and that silly little machine still works. *It’s like a happiness machine*.


**2) Star Trek**


There’s a new Star Trek movie, [Into Darkness](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1408101/), coming out starting on May 15th. Do not beware the ides of May.


**3) TBTF**


Sherrod Brown and David Vitter introduced an “end too-big-to-fail” bill this week and they wrote about it in [an Op-Ed for the New York Times](http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/24/opinion/make-wall-street-choose-go-small-or-go-home.html?smid=tw-nytopinion&_r=1&). It doesn’t mean it’ll be passed, or that it’s perfect, but the momentum is gaining, which is good.
