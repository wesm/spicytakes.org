I was recently interviewed by [Caroline Chen](http://www.linkedin.com/in/carolineylchen), a graduate student at Columbia’s Journalism School, about the status of Mochizuki’s proof the the ABC Conjecture. I think she found me through [my previous post on the subject](https://mathbabe.org/2012/11/14/the-abc-conjecture-has-not-been-proved/).


Anyway, [her article just came out](http://projectwordsworth.com/the-paradox-of-the-proof/), and I like it and wanted to share it, even though I don’t like the title (“The Paradox of the Proof”) because I don’t like the word *paradox* (when someone calls something a paradox, it means they are making an assumption that they don’t want to examine). But that’s just a pet peeve – the article is nice, and it features my buddies Moon and Jordan and my husband Johan.


Read the article [here](http://projectwordsworth.com/the-paradox-of-the-proof/).
