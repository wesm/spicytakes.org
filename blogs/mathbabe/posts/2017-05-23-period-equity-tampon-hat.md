I’ve gone and done it, folks: I’ve designed a “Period Equity (tampon) Hat” for my friend [Laura Strausfeld](http://www.feministlawprofessors.com/2016/11/interview-laura-strausfeld-new-york-attorney-challenging-tampon-tax/), who is speaking later today at a cool rally in D.C.:


[Rally for Safe Feminine Care Products in Washington, DC!](http://www.womensvoices.org/rally-for-safe-feminine-care-products-in-washington-dc/)


Anyway, here’s the hat, tell me what you think:


 
I had to learn a new technique called “intarsia in the round” in order to knit this hat. Also, I plan to put the design up on ravelry soon, so look for me there if you’re interested in knitting your own Period Equity (tampon) Hat! My Ravelry username is cathyoneil.
Also, if you’re wondering why I’m interested in this particular issue, and why Laura is speaking there, please read [this post](https://mathbabe.org/2016/03/03/hey-im-a-lead-plaintiff-on-the-nys-tampon-tax-case/), as well as [this one](https://mathbabe.org/2016/03/04/holy-shit-i-look-amazing-holding-tampons/), about how I was a plaintiff on the New York State tampon tax case, which [we won](https://mathbabe.org/2016/08/02/stuffs-going-on-some-of-its-progress/), and [Laura was the legal brain behind it.](http://www.feministlawprofessors.com/2016/11/interview-laura-strausfeld-new-york-attorney-challenging-tampon-tax/)
Laura has recently started an organization called [Period Equity](http://www.periodequity.org/) to further [the cause](http://www.alternet.org/personal-health/other-tampon-tax). And if you look at their site, you’ll see my hat design was pretty much a total rip-off of their website design.
