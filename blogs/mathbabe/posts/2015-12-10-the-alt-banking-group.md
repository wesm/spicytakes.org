My Occupy group, called [the Alternative Banking group](http://altbanking.net/), still meets every Sunday over at Columbia. Right now we’ve got a bunch of projects going on, including [a series of essays over at the Huffington Post](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/the-alternative-banking-group-of-ows/) and the continuation of [Occupy Summer School](http://altbanking.net/occupy-summer-school/) among others.


We also maintain [a reading list for our weekly discussions](http://altbanking.net/). It’s front and center on the landing page and consists of stories that people in the group find interesting, with archives going back almost two years.


Please [take a look](http://altbanking.net/), and consider bookmarking it if you think the reading lists are interesting. I know I waste my time every week reading lots of articles that are not nearly the quality of the ones listed on this reading list.


The rest of the website is up for a re-organization, and it’s by no means perfectly designed – some of it is outdated – but I think it’s already pretty amazing what the group has accomplished, and I’m proud to be part of it.
