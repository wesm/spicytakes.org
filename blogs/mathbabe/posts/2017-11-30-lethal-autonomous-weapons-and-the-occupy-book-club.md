**Book Club!**


I also wanted to announce that my [Occupy group Alt Banking](http://altbanking.net/) is starting a book club. We’re meeting this Sunday from 2-3pm at Columbia (room 409 of the International Affairs Building at Amsterdam and 118th), and we’re discussing the **introductions and first chapters** of the two following books:

1. [Capitalism and Freedom by Milton Friedman](http://circuloliberal.org/livros/capitalismo-e-liberdade.pdf), available for free online
2. [Capitalism and Slavery by Eric Williams](https://archive.org/stream/capitalismandsla033027mbp/capitalismandsla033027mbp_djvu.txt), which also seems to be available online.


Please join us, we welcome everyone and anyone!
