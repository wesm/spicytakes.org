The reviews are coming in of my new book, [Weapons of Math Destruction](https://www.amazon.com/Weapons-Math-Destruction-Increases-Inequality/dp/0553418815/ref=cm_cr_arp_d_product_top?ie=UTF8) (also available [as an audiobook](https://www.amazon.com/Weapons-Math-Destruction-Increases-Inequality/dp/B01JPAE44S/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1473247447&sr=8-1&keywords=weapons+of+math+destruction+audio), which I read myself! (clip [here](https://soundcloud.com/penguin-audio/weapons-of-math-destruction))). Here are some of them:

1. [Weapons of Math Destruction: invisible, ubiquitous algorithms are ruining millions of lives](http://boingboing.net/2016/09/06/weapons-of-math-destruction-i.html) by Cory Doctorow from his blog [Boingboing.net](http://boingboing.net)
2. [Weapons of Math Destruction](http://www.math.columbia.edu/~woit/wordpress/?p=8732) by Peter Woit from his blog, [Not Even Wrong](http://www.math.columbia.edu/~woit/wordpress/)
3. [Big Data Isn’t Just Watching You—It’s Making You Poorer](http://inthesetimes.com/article/19364/the-numbers-do-lie) by [Pankaj Mehta](http://inthesetimes.com/community/profile/322563) on the site [In These Times](http://inthesetimes.com/)
4. [Review: *Weapons of Math Destruction*](http://blogs.scientificamerican.com/roots-of-unity/review-weapons-of-math-destruction/) by Evelyn Lamb in Scientific American
5. [Math is racist: How data is driving inequality](http://money.cnn.com/2016/09/06/technology/weapons-of-math-destruction/index.html) by Aimee Rawlins on CNN Money. Note: I would not have chosen this title, since I’m not claiming math is racist, but rather that some potentially discriminatory practices are being shielded by mathematics. I should note that journalists don’t always choose their own titles, and I think Aimee did a good job with the content of the article
6. [On Cathy O’Neil’s Weapons of Math Destruction](https://hoofnagle.berkeley.edu/2016/09/02/on-cathy-oneils-weapons-of-math-destruction/) by Chris Hoofnagle, on [his blog at the UC Berkeley Law School](https://hoofnagle.berkeley.edu/)
7. [Math Is Biased Against Women and the Poor, According to a Former Math Professor](http://nymag.com/thecut/2016/09/cathy-oneils-weapons-of-math-destruction-math-is-biased.html) by Priya Rao on [NYMag’s The Cut](http://nymag.com/thecut/)


There may be more I’m missing, please send me links! The coverage has been fantastic, and I’m super excited for the coming weeks and months as we finally get to discuss these issues.


Also, I’d like to urge you all to r[eview my book on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Weapons-Math-Destruction-Increases-Inequality/product-reviews/0553418815/ref=pd_lutyp_simh_1_cr_1?ie=UTF8&refRID=WH22266FTWXP5FKST52X) when you get a chance. The book is controversial and a few negative reviews can drag down the average pretty quickly. Having said that, please be completely honest of course!
