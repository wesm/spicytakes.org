I’m very excited for the first day of [Occupy Summer School](http://altbanking.net/occupy-summer-school/). It will run for three weeks, every morning, in downtown Brooklyn, so I’m not sure how much blogging I’ll be doing. Hopefully I’ll find time to document some of what’s happening. We’re very excited about the line-up, and we have high hopes for the final event.


Today we introduce the kids to ourselves and Occupy, we talk about the next three weeks, we ask them to introduce themselves to us, we ask what they are planning to get out of the experience, and of course we eat donuts.
