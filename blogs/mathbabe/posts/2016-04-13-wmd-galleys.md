I’m super excited to announce that galleys for my book *Weapons of Math Destruction: how big data increases inequality and threatens democracy*, [available for pre-order on Amazon](http://www.amazon.com/Weapons-Math-Destruction-Increases-Inequality/dp/0553418815/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1460545851&sr=1-1&keywords=weapons+of+math+destruction), [or from Barnes and Noble](http://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/weapons-of-math-destruction-cathy-oneil/1123130166?ean=9780553418811), are now out.


Galleys aren’t the real book, they’re preliminary versions made explicitly for people who might want to review the book, who might want to blurb the book, or who will spread the word about how great it is.
