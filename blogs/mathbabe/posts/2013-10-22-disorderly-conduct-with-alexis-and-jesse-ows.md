**Podcast**


So there’s a new podcast called [Disorderly Conduct](https://www.tarbellnyc.com/discon) which “explores finance without a permit” and is hosted by [Alexis Goldstein](http://alexisgo.com/), whom I met through her work on Occupy the SEC, and [Jesse Myerson](http://jamyerson.com/), and activist and a writer.


I was recently a very brief guest on their [“In the Weeds” feature](https://www.tarbellnyc.com/discon), where I was asked to answer the question, “What is the single best way to rein in the power of Wall Street?” in three minutes. The answers given by:

1. me,
2. The Other 98% organizer Nicole Carty ([@nacarty](https://twitter.com/nacarty)),
3. Salon.com contributing writer David Dayen ([@ddayen](https://twitter.com/ddayen)),
4. Americans for Financial Reform Policy Director Marcus Stanley ([@MarcusMStanley](https://twitter.com/MarcusMStanley)), and
5. Marxist militant José Martín ([@sabokitty](https://twitter.com/sabokitty))


can be found [here](https://www.tarbellnyc.com/discon) or you can download the episode [here](https://www.tarbellnyc.com/podcasts/discon/discon_10142013.mp3).
