Great news! The [Alt Banking group](http://altbanking.net/) had a piece published today in the Huffington Post entitled *[With Economic Justice For All](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/the-alternative-banking-group-of-ows/with-economic-justice-for_b_6135850.html),* about our hopes for the next Attorney General.


For the sake of the essay, we coined the term “marble columns” to mean the opposite of “broken windows.” Instead of getting arrested for nothing, you never get arrested, as long as you work at a company with marble columns. For more, [take a look](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/the-alternative-banking-group-of-ows/with-economic-justice-for_b_6135850.html) at the whole piece!
