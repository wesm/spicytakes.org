I’m excited to announce that [Zephyr Teachout](http://www.zephyrteachout.com/speakout?splash=1), a Fordham Law School professor who is running against Andrew Cuomo for Governor of New York, will be coming to speak to the Alternative Banking group next Sunday, July 13th, from 3pm-5pm in the usual place, Room 409 of the International Affairs Building at 118th and Amsterdam. More about Alt Banking on [our website](http://altbanking.net/).


**Title**: Teachout-Wu vs. Cuomo-Hochul in the Democratic Primary in New York!


**Description**: Come hear candidate Teachout talk about her anti-corruption trust-busting campaign against Governor Cuomo.


**Background**: Teachout is an antitrust and media expert who served as the Director of Internet organizing for the 2004 [Howard Dean](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Howard_Dean) Presidential Campaign. She co-founded [A New Way Forward](http://www.anewwayforward.org/), an organization built to break up the power of big banks. Teachout was the first national director of the [Sunlight Foundation](http://sunlightfoundation.com/). More [here](http://law.fordham.edu/faculty/zephyrteachout.htm).


If we have time after talking to Zephyr we will discuss Stiglitz’s article,  [The Myth Of America’s Golden Age](http://www.politico.com/magazine/story/2014/06/the-myth-of-americas-golden-age-108013.html?ml=m_ms#.U7mwrI1dX3X).


Please make time to come hear Zephyr, and please spread the word.
