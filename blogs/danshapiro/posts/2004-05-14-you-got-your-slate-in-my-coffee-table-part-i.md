[Here are the blueprints](http://www.danshapiro.com/blog/archives/Coffee Table with Slate Inset - Copyright 2004 Dan Shapiro.pdf) for my first major furniture project, our living room coffee table.  This will be broken out over several posts.  Yes, my drawings are horrible.  The profs who taught me proper [GD&T](http://www.efunda.com/DesignStandards/gdt/introduction.cfm) will kick my ass when they see it.  Still, it was good enough to print out, scatter copies of around the shop, spill shellac on, and ultimately produce a table from.

Later posts will talk about construction, assembly, and finishing.
