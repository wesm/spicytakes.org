I just got a very nice email from [Danielle Fong](http://daniellefong.com ), the 25 year old middle school-dropout, cofounder and chief scientist of [Lightsail Energy](http://www.lightsail.com/).


That’s not the amazing part.


Lightsail makes regenerative brakes for the power grid.  You know how your* Prius takes the power from the brakes when you don’t need it, and dumps it back in to the engine when you do need it?  Lightsail does the same thing for energy plants that can’t scale their production quickly with the needs of consumers, not to mention greentech like wind and solar that creates power when Willard Scott says so.


Still not the amazing part.


So where are the startups you know headquartered?  Someplace cool and funky, right?  We put Ontela in the [Smith Tower](http://www.smithtower.com/), the tallest building west of the Mississippi (until the disrespectful hooligans in the midwest built the [Kansas City Power & Light Building](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kansas_City_Power_%26_Light_Building) in 1931).  It was fantastic!  But Lightsail?  Forget that.  Check out where *they* call home.


![](http://einfall.files.wordpress.com/2012/01/firehouse-500wd1.jpg)

*The Lightsail Firehouse*


Oh yeah.  Remind you of anything?


![](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/Ghostbusters_Firehouse.jpg/800px-Ghostbusters_Firehouse.jpg)

*The Ghostbusters Firehouse*


And you know what?  We’re *still *not at the mind blowingly awesome part yet.  Because you know what they have in the basement?


Oh yes.


Bravo, Lightsail.  Bravo.


**Urgent Addendum:**


Danielle informed me after publication of this article that they have vacated the Ghostbusters firehouse and moved to a chocolate factory.


I rest my case.
