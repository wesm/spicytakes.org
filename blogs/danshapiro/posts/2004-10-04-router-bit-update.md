A quick update to my [earlier post](http://www.danshapiro.com/blog/archives/000056a_router_bit_review_that_isnt_an_insult_to_your_intelligence.html) about router bit reviews.  I got one of the Oldham Viper bits that was recommended there–a nice fat 3/4 straight bit.  Used it to trim some 5/4 walnut, put rabbets in maple, and a few other tasks.  The bad boy makes sawdust like a woodchuck.  It gives a noticably better finish than the El Cheapo Grande carbide bits I have already, too.

Pick up a few.  You’ll thank me for it.
