Just ordered a copy of [eCabinet Systems](http://www.ecabinetsystems.com).  My neighbor, a semiprofessional woodworker, recommends it highly.  Evidently it lets you design your cabinets in a CAD environment, then shows you renderings of the finished cabinets, lets you order all the hardware wholesale, produces cutlists, and ultimately creates files you can send to have the wood milled for you.  Looks promising.  I’ll post when I know better how it works, but for the moment you can check out this [review](http://www.woodistry.com/ecabs_review.htm).
