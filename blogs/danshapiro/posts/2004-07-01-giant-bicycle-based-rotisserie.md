Although this project was metal, not wood, and most of the interesting engineering was done by my friend Frank, [this is worth sharing](http://www.frankschmitt.org/contentbuilder/layout.php3?contentPath=content/00/01/08/67/84/userdirectory72.content).  Basically, Frank built a 6′ long rotisserie out of a couple of bicycles, a washing machine motor, and some stainless steel.  We used it to cook 140 lbs of lamb for my wedding, which came out perfectly.
