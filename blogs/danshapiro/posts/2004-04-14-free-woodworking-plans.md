I’ll be darned.  [Freeplans.com](http://www.freeplans.com) actually appears not to be a rip-off.  Their deal is pretty simple: it’s a list that publishes free woodworking plans, with advertisements.  They promise not to sell your name for anything else, and to make it easy to sign off the list.  I just got the first plan, and it was high-quality step-by-step instructions on how to make a cherry bookcase.  Pictures, clear instructions, the works.  I’m impressed!


(I used a “tearoff” email address, so I’ll know if they sell my name.  Basically, it’s a one-shot email address that forwards to my regular address–if I start getting spam, I can know where it came from and delete the email address, ending the spam).
