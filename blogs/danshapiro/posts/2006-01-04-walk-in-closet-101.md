Lars Liden did a ton of work to redo his walk-in closet, including building an outdoor hutch for his hot water heater.  It’s more a home-improvement than a woodworking project, but it’s still worthy of note because he has detailed pictures of the whole thing.

http://flickr.com/photos/larsliden/sets/1737556/

One note: if you build a project with movable shelves, instead of painstakingly locating each shelf pin hole, use a shop-made shelf pin hole jig like [this one at Benchnotes](http://www.benchnotes.com/Shelf%20Hole%20Guide/shelf_hole_drilling_jig.htm) or [this one at Woodworking Tips](http://www.woodworkingtips.com/etips/etip122900wb.html).

You’ll thank me for it.
