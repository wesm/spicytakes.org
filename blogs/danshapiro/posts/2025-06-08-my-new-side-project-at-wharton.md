(Yes, the day job is still Glowforge and lasers, and I still love it!)


I’ve signed on as a research fellow at Wharton’s [Generative AI Lab](https://gail.wharton.upenn.edu/about-us/) with the incredible Drs Ethan and Lilach Mollick.


I’m working with them to share some of the the lessons we’ve learned building generative AI tools at Glowforge so more people can put them to work. And I’m excited to investigate some of the strange and truly bizarre phenomena I’ve been seeing in the world of LLMs.


I know the blog’s been quiet… guilty. I’ll be sharing some of the interesting tidbits soon!
