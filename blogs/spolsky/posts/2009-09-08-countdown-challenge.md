This month we’re starting to get organized for [StackOverflow DevDays](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/items/2009/05/12.html), a series of one-day, mini conferences in ten different cities. Because of the packed schedule, keeping on time is very important, so I want to have a full-screen countdown application that we can run during intermissions warning people when we’re going to resume. We’ll put this up on the main screen and hopefully that will encourage people to sit down and be quiet on time. I thought this would be a great opportunity to showcase the StackOverflow community’s programming talent, so I posted a little contest over on [meta](http://meta.stackoverflow.com/questions/20420/countdown-app-for-devdays).
