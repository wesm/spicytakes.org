Want to know how to [export mail from Gmail](http://webapps.stackexchange.com/questions/417/can-i-export-gmail-messages-to-text-or-html-files)? Or [delete your Facebook account](http://webapps.stackexchange.com/questions/1/how-do-i-delete-my-facebook-account)? Or [send giant files via email](http://webapps.stackexchange.com/questions/1062/how-to-send-big-files-via-email)?


Well, the new [Web Applications Stack Exchange](http://webapps.stackexchange.com/) is for you. It’s a part of the Stack Exchange network, so it has the clean, elegant design that made Stack Overflow [a phenomenal success](http://www.quantcast.com/p-c1rF4kxgLUzNc).


The newest member of the Stack Exchange Network is the first one to go through the community site-creation process called [Area 51](http://area51.stackexchange.com/). There are more great sites in the pipeline, but they have to demonstrate that they can reach critical mass or we won’t create them.
