The last few days before shipping a product should be extremely quiet, as the rate of incoming bugs approaches zero. That’s where we are now. I’ve been putting the final touches on a new version of Fog Creek’s website (you can see a sneak preview [here](http://new.fogcreek.com)) and taking the word “Beta” out of the code wherever it appears. We’re likely to go live this week — exactly according to the [schedule](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000245.html) we set for ourselves six months ago.
