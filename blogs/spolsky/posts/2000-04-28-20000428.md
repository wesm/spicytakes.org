I’m coming to suspect that Microsoft has this one extra marketing team that is not assigned to a product. Their job is to make up a non-thing and market the heck out of it. This year it’s [NGWS](http://www3.zdnet.com/zdnn/stories/news/0,4586,2556472-1,00.html). Last year it was DNA. The year before, it was ActiveX. Before that, it was Intellisense. Even before Intellisense, they had WOSA. What all these have in common is that they don’t exist and don’t mean anything, but they have a marketing team from Microsoft blathering about them.
