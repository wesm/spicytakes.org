[Jakob Nielsen says](http://www.useit.com/alertbox/20001029.html) that Flash is “99% bad.” I have to agree. Flash *always* reduces usability. On the other hand, every time I read Jakob Nielsen, I get this feeling that he really doesn’t appreciate that usability is not the most important thing on earth. Sure, usability is important (I wrote a [whole book](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/uibook/chapters/fog0000000057.html) about it). But it is simply not everyone’s number one priority, nor should it be. You get the feeling that if Mr Nielsen designed a singles bar, it would be well lit, clean, with giant menus printed in Arial 14 point, and you’d never have to wait to get a drink. But nobody would go there, they would all be at [Coyote Ugly Saloon](http://www.bway.net/~kfitz/coyote.htm) pouring beer on each other.
