[Fogcreek.de](http://www.fogcreek.de/index.html) is now completely live, offering full-service sales and support of [FogBugz in German](http://www.fogcreek.de/FogBugz/index.html) from Munich. FogBugz itself works in English or German, based on the user’s choice.


This is our first real international distributor, and a major milestone for Fog Creek Software. If you’re using FogBugz in Germany, Austria, or Switzerland, [give Branimir a call](http://www.fogcreek.de/Contact.html) to introduce yourself.
