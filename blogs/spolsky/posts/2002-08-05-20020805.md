Science fiction writer Bruce Sterling [on open source](http://www.viridiandesign.org/notes/301-350/00325_open_source_speech.html): “‘Don’t like it? Hey, just reconfigure it yourself, don’t bother me!’ It’s the Hippie Squat Model of software architecture. ‘If I want to paint the doors and floors bright blue and put the toilet right into the kitchen, why not?’ It’s very offensive to user sensibilities and it is as ugly as a sack full of penguin guts.”

We sent off our first batch of [affiliate payment](http://www.fogcreek.com/Affiliates.html) checks. Yes, people are making money linking to Joel on Software. I hadn’t even noticed it, but CityDesk got [mentioned](http://www.nytimes.com/2002/07/25/technology/circuits/25ASKK.html) in the New York Times a couple of weeks ago.
