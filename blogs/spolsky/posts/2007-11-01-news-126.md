I’ll be speaking at the [Irish Java Technology Conference](http://ijtc.firstport.ie/) on November 7th, in Dublin.


[Stefan](http://www.fogcreek.com/FogBugz/blog/post/The-Origin-of-Wasabi.aspx) has started a series of articles about Wasabi: “During the summer of 2006, we realized that I was a complete dork for naming it FogBasic, so we had a naming contest. Everyone submitted their name suggestions, and we did instant run-off voting to decide the name we would actually use. The final two were Bone Crusher 3000 and Wasabi. As much as I wanted to be typing bc3k into the command line, Wasabi won out.”
