It seems like Stack Exchange is the perfect platform for questions about Jewish observance. After all, most of the Talmud reads just like [Stack Overflow](http://stackoverflow.com): a question, followed by multiple answers, usually with the highest ranking answer appearing first. The number of questions is infinite.


If you would be interested in participating in such a thing, please commit to[ the proposal](http://area51.stackexchange.com/proposals/22435/jewish-life-and-learning?referrer=2otMTfv6kTY1) today.
