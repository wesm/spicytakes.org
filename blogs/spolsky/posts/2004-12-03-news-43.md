See that little picture of the books on the left hand side? It used to be 42,241 bytes long. 34,885 of those bytes were in a useless “application block” that some photo editing program put there. Thanks to [Dennis Forbes](http://www.yafla.com/papers/purejpeg/filter_unnecessary_jpeg_info_such_as_exif.htm), who posted an explanation and a free utility to remove the unneeded bloat, it’s now only 7354 bytes.
