[Ev](http://www.evhead.com/) has a neat idea for [an application](http://www.evhead.com/longer/2943130_essays.asp) that he calls Alias Manager. It’s a simple way to deal with having a team of people who are responsible for answering emails to one email alias, like the world famous [president@whitehouse.gov](mailto:president@whitehouse.gov), or the almost as famous [customer-support@fogcreek.com](mailto:customer-support@fogcreek.com). When I read his idea, I thought, *smart Ev!* We need that too! Then I realized that [FogBUGZ](http://www.fogcreek.com/FogBUGZ) *almost* does it, but not quite. It would take about $10,000 of investment to add this feature to FogBUGZ. So I thought I’d ask my readers two questions:  have you seen a simple, cheap application that does this?  do you need a simple, cheap application that does this? Postscript: I’ve gotten tons of [replies](http://www.greenspun.com/bboard/q-and-a-fetch-msg.tcl?msg_id=0059RM). There are a lot of expensive CRM packages. That’s not what I’m talking about, they are too complicated and too expensive for small business. There are also some open source things, many people mentioned [rt](http://www.fsck.com/projects/rt/).
