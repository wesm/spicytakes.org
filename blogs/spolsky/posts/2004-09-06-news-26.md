**My Latest Article**


Software in the 1980s, when usability was “invented,” was all about computer-human interaction. A lot of software still is. But the Internet brings us a new kind of software: software that’s about human-human interaction. When you’re writing software that mediates between people, after you get the usability right, you have to get the social interface right. And the social interface is *more important*. The best UI in the world won’t save software with an awkward social interface. [It’s Not Just Usability](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/NotJustUsability.html)
