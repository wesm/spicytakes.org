I’m pretty psyched, because [UI Design for Programmers](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/uibook/chapters/fog0000000057.html) is going to appear in print soon.  The print version will be expanded from the web version, with about 5 bonus chapters, lots more illustrations, and a foreword by [Dave Winer](http://dave.editthispage.com/myNameIsDaveWiner). It’s being published by [Apress](http://www.apress.com), a publisher I love because the founder, Gary Cornell, actually knows more about programming than I do, a rare trait in a computer books publisher.
