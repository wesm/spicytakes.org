I spent the day catching up on the Joel on Software translation effort.  There are new articles online in Danish, Dutch, French, German, Hungarian, Indonesian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazilian and Iberian), and Romanian.  As my faithful volunteers have learned it sometimes takes me a week to respond to their email; I usually let all the translation-related email pile up and then work through it once a week. Right now there are a few articles stuck in the [queue](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/LanguageSignup.html) because they don’t have a copy editor — if you can edit in Farsi, Portuguese (Portugal), Russian, Spanish, or Turkish, please [let me know](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/navLinks/fog0000000263.html)!
