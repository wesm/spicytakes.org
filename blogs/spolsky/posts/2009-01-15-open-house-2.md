After I posted [pictures](http://picasaweb.google.com/spolsky/FogCreekSNewOffice#) of the [new Fog Creek office](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/items/2008/12/29.html), a lot of people wrote to ask if they could visit it. So we’re holding an open house here on Friday, January 30th, from 12:00-1:00 pm (absolutely no one can be admitted after 1:00, sorry). There will be free food and drinks.


Fog Creek is at 55 Broadway, New York, on the 25th floor.
