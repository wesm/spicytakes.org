Version 5.0 of Microsoft’s flagship spreadsheet program Excel came out in 1993. It was positively *huge*: it required a whole *15 megabytes* of hard drive space. In those days we could still remember our first 20MB PC hard drives (around 1985) and so 15MB sure seemed like a lot. By the time Excel 2000 came out, it required a whopping 146MB … almost a tenfold increase! Dang those sloppy Microsoft programmers, right?[Wrong.](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000020.html)
