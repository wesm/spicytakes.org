[Laura Frieder and Jonathan Zittrain](http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=920553): “The average investor who buys a stock on the day it is most heavily touted and sells it 2 days after the touting ends will lose approximately 5.5%. For the top half of most thoroughly touted stocks, … a spammer who buys at the ask price on the day before unleashing touts and sells at the bid price on the day his or her touting is the heaviest will, on average, earn 5.79%.”


Leonard Richardson has a site which [monitors daily stock spam activity](http://www.crummy.com/features/StockSpam/). You can see from his charts that buying these scammy penny stocks is a guaranteed way to lose money.
