Another thing users have trouble with is using the mouse. Even if they are adept mousers, using the mouse precisely can be difficult.  [User Interface Design for Programmers 7](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/uibook/chapters/fog0000000063.html) talks about how to smooth out some of the hard edges of your program, by thinking about the dexterity it takes to use the mouse in UI.
