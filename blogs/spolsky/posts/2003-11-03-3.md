Fog Creek’s website has been [redone](http://www.fogcreek.com) to use our new Sam Sherwood-designed logo. The cutting edge page design is thanks to superstar web designer [Dave Shea](http://www.mezzoblue.com/), famous for the [CSS garden](http://www.csszengarden.com/) and the eye-popping [new Mozilla home page](http://website-beta.mozilla.org/), with additional programming and graphics by Fog Creek’s own Dmitri Kalmar. It’s about 99% standards-compliant (with the exception of a couple of stray FONT tags left over from old content that hasn’t been updated… oh the horror!).
