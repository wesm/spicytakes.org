[Philip Greenspun](http://philip.greenspun.com/) has been a key inspiration to many people, including Fog Creek. We owe him a debt. He started a company called arsDigita, which was really just another consulting company for the Internet, but it had two things which made it uniquely different. It had a **personal voice, **and it had **education**. But now it doesn’t have Philip, and that’s a real bummer.[ Spring in Cambridge](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000021.html)
