We gave all the Fog Creek programmers a choice of  which conference to attend this spring, from a long list  of about 12 conferences, and all of them wanted to go to  [ETech](http://conferences.oreillynet.com/etech/).  So that’s where you’ll find us next March. It was incredibly  interesting last year — most of the presentations, especially the ones that weren’t given by the sponsors, were great, but the best part was interacting with the interesting attendees.
