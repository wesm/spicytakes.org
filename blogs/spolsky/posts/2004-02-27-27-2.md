[On software pricing](http://discuss.fogcreek.com/newyork/default.asp?cmd=show&ixPost=1389&ixReplies=7): “StartFragment With software sold in corporations, as soon as your price gets up in the $3000 level, the amount of approval it needs is so absurd that you are not going to sell products without a salesperson making a few visits. That’s why you see a lot of software products at $100,000 and a lot under $3000, but anywhere in-between and it’s impossible to make sales. ”
