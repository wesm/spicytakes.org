[Steve Krug](http://www.sensible.com/) has written a follow up to his usability classic [*Don’t Make Me Think*](http://www.sensible.com/dmmt.html)*. *The sequel, [*Rocket Surgery Made Easy*](http://www.sensible.com/rocketsurgery/index.html)*, *is a terrific, short, concise, fun guide to running simple “hallway” usability tests to improve the usability of your software and websites. Highly recommended.
