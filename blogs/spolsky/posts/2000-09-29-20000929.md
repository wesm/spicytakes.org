This is strange.  In 1960, almost 40 years before the Internet came along, Barbra Streisand [drops](http://www.britannica.com/bcom/eb/article/0/0,5716,71770+1+69949,00.html?kw=barbra%20streisand) the “a” from her first name.  Of course, with the unusual spelling, it’s *much* easier to find her in search engines, on Amazon, etc.  That woman has incredible foresight.
