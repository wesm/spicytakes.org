   Dave Thomas 1932-2002     Dave Thomas, the founder of Wendy’s, passed away today. Appearing in hundreds of commercials, he gave his hamburger chain a friendly, down-home feel. “Mr. Thomas came to stand for the products he peddled because he was perceived as standing behind them, as if a curious customer could peek inside the kitchen at a Wendy’s restaurant and perhaps find Mr. Thomas keeping a watchful eye on the cook at the grill,” wrote [Stuart Elliot](http://www.nytimes.com/2002/01/09/business/media/09ADCO.html) in the New York Times. He taught us that nobody, given the choice, prefers to buy from a faceless megacorporation.
