 I have to give two speeches at [CFUNITED](http://www.cfunited.com/), the ColdFusion technical conference coming up in Washington DC June 29th – July 1st. The second is entitled “Project Management the *Joel on Software* Way.” I’ll mostly be going over [The Joel Test](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000043.html), but at the end I’ll leave time for a demo of FogBugz 4.0.
Michael Smith, who is organizing CFUNITED, [interviewed me](http://www.cfunited.com/interview_display.cfm?TopicId=181).
