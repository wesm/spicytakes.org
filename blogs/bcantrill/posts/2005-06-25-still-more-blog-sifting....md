When we first tried to encourage Solaris engineers to blog on Opening Day, I thought we were going to have a hard time convincing engineers to blog – I knew that providing in-depth, technical content takes a lot of time, and I knew that everyone had other priorities. So when we were planning the launch and talking about the possibility of dealing with a massive amount of Opening Day content, my response was “hurt me with that problem.” Well, as it turns out, most engineers didn’t need much convincing – many provided rich, deep content – and I was indeed hurt with that problem!
