Cindy is very conscious of good workmanship in carpentry. She'll
	notice all sorts of fine details that I'm oblivious of. She
	particularly appreciates things that don't look like much, but are
	actually quite tricky to do right.


I noticed such a fine detail this morning, but in software -
	specifically Apple's iTunes. I was sorting out some tags on my music
	collection. Part of this was cleaning up my messy tags of
	Beethoven. As I was doing this I saw that iTunes sorted 'Piano
	Sonata No.8 in Cm' before 'Piano Sonata No.10 in G'. Someone,
	somewhere cared enough to make that happen - I appreciate it.
