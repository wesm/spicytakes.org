I have a couple of feeds for those who like to use them. Although
	I've used the term RSS Feed here, they are actually [Atom](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atom_(standard)) feeds.


The [updates
feed](https://martinfowler.com/updates.atom) covers changes to my web site - it's the same things as you
can see in the âRecent Updatesâ section of my [home page](https://martinfowler.com/). It's pretty low volume
as web site updates are spotty at best. This is the one to pick if you
want to know about site updates but don't care to read my blog.


The [bliki
feed](https://martinfowler.com/bliki/bliki.atom) contains the new items on my blog (what appears on the [bliki page](https://martinfowler.com/bliki)). It also includes
general site updates from the updates feed so people who want
everything can do so with a single feed instead of having to subscribe
to two feeds.


Another way to subscribe to my feeds is to subscribe to the
[ThoughtBlogs feed](http://www.thoughtworks.com/blogs), which contains all the various blogs by
ThoughtWorkers. This feed syndicates my bliki feed. There are quite a
few other feeds that syndicate my feeds, but I'm afraid I don't keep
track of them.


I added the atom feeds on December 27th 2006. Before then I had
	feeds in RSS 0.91. Currently those feeds are still there, but
	deprecated. I will remove them if I ever have to put any effort to
	keep them running.
