From time to time I run into people who want to get a smalltalk
and give it a spin to see what the fuss is about. My old [favorite introductory smalltalk book](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0133713458/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=0133713458&linkCode=as2&tag=martinfowlerc-20) went out
of print, but I just discovered you can now [download it
from here](http://www.iam.unibe.ch/~ducasse/FreeBooks.html) together with lots of other smalltalk related material.
The material is hosted by Stéphane Ducasse, who was a co-author on an
excellent book on [reengineering
patterns](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1558606394/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=1558606394&linkCode=as2&tag=martinfowlerc-20).
