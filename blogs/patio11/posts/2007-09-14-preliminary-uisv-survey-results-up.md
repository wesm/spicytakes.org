Somebody on the Business of Software forums took it upon themselves to survey 96 uISVs on a variety of topics.  The preliminary results of the [microISV survey](http://blog.businessofsoftware.org/2007/09/micro-isv-sur-1.html), primarily about demographics, are now posted on their blog.  While I think that most folks are waiting with bated breath for the sales results, anybody who does that much work for the community deserves links early and often.  (Hint, hint for all you bloggers out there.)
