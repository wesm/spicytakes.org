When I started this blog four years ago, I never thought it would be seen by more than a few dozen people, so I never planned any sort of information architecture to it.  500 posts (300,000 words!) and several hundred thousand readers later, it is unwieldy to get to the good stuff unless you have been following along for the last couple of years, or have a week free.  To make this a little less annoying, with the help of an assistant from Hacker News I found the [best 72 articles I’ve written](https://www.kalzumeus.com/greatest-hits/), grouped them by category, and for ones which are logically related explained what the connections are (in particular, for the “experiment” / “results” pairs).


I hope you find it useful.  If I let out anything or if there is a category you know I publish on but did not include in the list, please let me know in the comments.
