Biggest Coup This Month: [Snowflake queries](http://microisvjournal.wordpress.com/2007/05/30/ranking-for-an-arbitrary-organic-search-query/).  I rewrote [one page of mine](http://www.bingocardcreator.com/printable-bingo-cards.htm), which had previously been very underused, to snap up more of them.  Handily, that rewrite was completed on May 30th.  During June, that page saw 5,600 page views (or about 27% of my total).  During May, that was 2,700 page views, or 12% of my total.  The “extra” 3,000 page views were a major part of the reason that, despite the fact traffic on my major teacher-related search terms declined since teachers are out of school, my overall traffic stayed mostly flat while my sales increased markedly.  I can’t wait to get some time to write some more content and catch myself some more snowflakes.
