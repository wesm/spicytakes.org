When folks ask me “So how exactly did your uISV come about?”, I generally tell them to go to the start of the blog and start reading.  Unfortunately, blog software is generally designed to be read from most recent to oldest.  Anyhow, a gentleman named Rizal mailed me today and said he created a “[MicroISV on a Shoestring Reader](http://www.rizalalmashoor.com/reader/microisvjournal/)“, which is a mashup that displays the blog organized in chronological order.  Its simple and effective.  Thanks a bunch, I’m sure some folks will find it useful.
