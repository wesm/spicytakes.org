Honestly I can’t stand k8s. It’s a simplified clone of Google’s internal borg system, but the learning curve is still just gross. It forces you to learn all these concepts that you shouldn’t need to worry about up front. Compared to something like Amazon Fargate or HashiCorp Nomad, k8s’s design is overcomplicated and (at least last I checked) not as scalable. I think the battle for container orchestration is *far* from over, and I’m not a big believer in the future of k8s.
