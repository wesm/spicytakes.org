Now that Apple has [announced a ship date](http://www.macminute.com/2005/04/12/tiger/) (April 29) for Mac OS X 10.4 Tiger, Amazon.com has made the family pack 5-user version [available for pre-order](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/asin/B0007LW1MW/ref=nosim/daringfirebal-20) as well. (Up until today, you could only pre-order the single-user version.)


Amazon’s product page for the family pack doesn’t mention it, but the [rebate form here](http://images.amazon.com/images/P/B0002G71T0.01.RB10.LZZZZZZZ.jpg), which is linked from the product page for the single-user version of Tiger, can save you $50. So, just like with the single-user version, buying the Tiger family pack via the links at the top of this page will both save you some money and help support Daring Fireball via Amazon’s filthy associates lucre.
