[Daring Fireball T-shirts are available once again](https://daringfireball.net/members/), for two reasons:

1. I have a few dozen extras remaining from the [first run](https://daringfireball.net/2004/06/something_daring).
2. I’ve been getting a steady stream of email from readers asking
if they can still buy shirts.


Current stock will be shipped first-come, first-served. So if you order
quickly, you’ll get one of the remaining shirts from the first run. The
rest will require a second printing run. Within the next week or so,
I’ll make a last-call announcement before pulling the plug, and I’ll
then order the second batch of shirts. As they say on TV: act now.


I’ve changed the pricing slightly: the shirts are now $29 USD; shipping
within the United States and Canada is free, add $5 for shipping
anywhere else in the world. (Also note that ladies’ size shirts are no
longer available.)


As before, everyone who purchases a shirt also gets a one-year
membership to Daring Fireball. Perks include access to [members-only RSS
feeds](https://daringfireball.net/members/info), plus more fun stuff coming soon. If you’re a new reader, you
can [read the original announcement from June](https://daringfireball.net/2004/06/something_daring) for my thinking
behind using a paid membership drive to support Daring Fireball.


To everyone who’s already become a member: my sincere thanks.


## Upgrades


Anyone who has already purchased a Daring Fireball membership for $25 is
eligible to purchase a shirt at a significant discount:

- If you live in the U.S. or Canada: $6.
- Otherwise: $11.


Pay the appropriate amount using the membership [donation form](https://daringfireball.net/members/),
then send an email to [support@daringfireball.net](mailto:support@daringfireball.net) with your shirt size
and shipping address. (You can use this email address for any questions
regarding the shirts, as well.) If you purchased a membership for $20.48
during the initial membership drive, you can upgrade by adding $4 to the
above prices.
