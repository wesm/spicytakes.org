Matthew “mpt” Thomas has a [new weblog](http://mpt.net.nz/), running the intriguing new [WordPress](http://wordpress.org/) weblogging software, and a new iBook, running the intriguing new Mac OS X operating system.


It’s always fun when a thoughtful usability critic switches from Mac OS 9 to OS X, and Thomas is no exception. His initial reaction: “[My first 48 hours enduring Mac OS X](http://mpt.net.nz/archive/2004/02/16/os-x)”.


Out of his 48 points, I disagree with one and a half — all of #28, and the parenthetical aside in #3.


Recommended reading, even if you wind up disagreeing with more
than 1.5 of his criticisms.
