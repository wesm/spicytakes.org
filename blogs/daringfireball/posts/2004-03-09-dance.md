[SmartyPants 1.5](https://daringfireball.net/projects/smartypants/) is out. Just a couple of minor improvements —
plus one more thing: integration with a new plug-in, coming later
today next week. Check the [release notes](https://daringfireball.net/projects/smartypants/#version_1.5.1) if you can’t wait
for the next announcement, or if you want to be able to tell your
grandchildren you were in on it from before it was actually announced.


**Update:** [Version 1.5.1 is out](https://daringfireball.net/projects/smartypants/). The one and only change is a
fix for a bug that caused a blank menu item to appear in your Movable
Type Text Formatting pop-up menu, if you had SmartyPants 1.5.0
installed, but did *not* have my technically-not-yet-announced new
text formatting plug-in installed as well. Sorry for the goof.
