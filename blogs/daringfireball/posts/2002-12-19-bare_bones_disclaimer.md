I have a bunch of BBEdit-related articles in the works for the next week. So now seems like a good time to once again disclaim my relationship with Bare Bones Software. For the record, I worked for Bare Bones from October 2000 through July 2002. I no longer work for them, and my comments on their software do not reflect anyone’s opinion but my own.


The best way I can say it is this: Having worked for them is not the reason why I’m such a fan of their software; rather, it’s because I like their software that I wanted to work for them.
