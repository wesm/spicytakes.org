Brent Simmons has generously offered a full license to the award-winning [NetNewsWire](http://ranchero.com/netnewswire/) 1.x for my use in promoting the Daring Fireball membership
drive. And since the upcoming [NetNewsWire 2.0 will be a free
update](http://inessential.com/?comments=1&postid=2848), this license will work with version 2.0 as well, after it’s
released.


To boot, I’ve got a couple of [Gmail](http://gmail.google.com/) invitations burning a hole
in my pocket. [**Update, 22 June:** I now have a staggering 25 Gmail
invitations to give away; thanks to Jon Gales of [MacMerc](http://macmerc.com/),
Larry Angell of [MacMinute](http://macminute.com/), and numerous others for sending me
extra invitations. **Update, 23 June:** The Gmail invitations have all
been awarded; the winners were notified by email.]


Here’s how it’ll work. The NetNewsWire license will be awarded July
1 to one random member (someone who’s donated $20.48 or more, or
who’s bought a T-shirt) *after* the membership drive is over. If
you’ve already joined, you’re already entered.


The Gmail invitations, on the other hand, I’m going to give away at
some point in the evening, Tuesday, 22 June. Anyone who’s donated
any amount whatsoever is eligible for the Gmail account giveaway. Thus,
[donate today](https://daringfireball.net/members/) if you want a chance at them.
