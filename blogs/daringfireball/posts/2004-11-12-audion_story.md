Ordinarily, this is the sort of thing I’d post to the [Linked List](https://daringfireball.net/linked/), but it’s simply too good — extraordinarily, wonderfully good — and thus deserves your full attention.


Get yourself a lovely beverage, disconnect the phone, quit your IM client, and enjoy [The True Story of Audion](http://panic.com/extras/audionstory/), by Cabel Sasser of Panic Software. One of the greatest Mac stories I’ve ever read. Joyous, exciting, heartbreaking. Note: most of the links are pop-up footnotes; don’t skip them.


Seriously, read this. You’ll thank me. Plus, there will be a quiz next week.
