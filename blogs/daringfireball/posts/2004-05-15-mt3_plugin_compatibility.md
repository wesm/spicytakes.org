In the incredibly unlikely case that you haven’t heard, 
[Six Apart has released a “developer edition” of Movable Type 3.0](http://www.movabletype.org/news/2004_05.shtml#001048).


The current versions of both of my MT plug-ins — [SmartyPants](https://daringfireball.net/projects/smartypants/) and 
[Markdown](https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/) — are fully compatible with MT 3.0.
