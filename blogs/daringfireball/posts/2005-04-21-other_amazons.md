Those of you in Canada or the U.K., take note: the “pre-order Mac OS X Tiger from Amazon” links atop this site now (finally) respect the Amazon location specified on the [preferences](https://daringfireball.net/preferences/) page. I.e. if you specify Amazon.ca or Amazon.co.uk as your Amazon store of choice, the promotional links will reflect your choice.


The discounts aren’t quite as sweet as those offered by Amazon.com here in the U.S., but they still beat the prices from the Canadian and U.K. Apple Stores.


My thanks to everyone who has already ordered their copies of Tiger through these links; it’s a great way to support this site and save yourself a few bucks at the same time.
