So Netscape 7.0 is [out](http://channels.netscape.com/ns/browsers/download.jsp).
Does anyone care? If so, please let me know why anyone in their
right mind would want to use Netscape 7 instead of Mozilla 1.1.


The best and most-popular browser feature in Mozilla is its ability to block pop-up ads. And, of course, Netscape *[removed](http://www.mozillazine.org/weblogs/hyatt/2002_08_25_mozillian_archive.html#85395270)* this feature from its browser. Wait, that’s not all. Not only did they remove the ad-blocking — a feature that is popular because people are annoyed by stupid ads — but they junked up the browser UI by adding a bunch of their own ads for crappy AOL services.


This isn’t just preposterously dumb — it’s sad. Especially so if you remember when Netscape was cool, back when a major new version of Netscape meant that the entire web instantly became a better place.
