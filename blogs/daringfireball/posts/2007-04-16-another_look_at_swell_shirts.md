## What You Should Do With Your Daring Fireball T-Shirt


Any high-profile  situation where you need to look your best is a good time to wear your DF t-shirt. One highlight from last year came when reader [Josh Dickens wore his DF shirt](http://daringfireball.net/linked/2006/july#fri-14-df_bobby_flay) to a taping of the Food Network’s “Bobby Flay’s Throwdown”. Another all-time favorite was [Eric Case taking his DF shirt](http://vedana.net/2004/10/something-daring.shtml) to the 20,000-foot peak of Stok Kangri in the Indian Himalayas.


Here’s another example. Let’s say you’re a successful screenwriter who has written and directed a film that’s playing at Sundance. What do you wear for the promotional photos? [John August wore his DF t-shirt.](http://imdb.com/title/tt0810988/photogallery)


(August’s movie, *The Nines*, looks very clever and got [great reviews](http://slashfilm.com/2007/01/23/sundance-the-nines-movie-review/), and he writes an [excellent weblog about screenwriting](http://johnaugust.com/).)
