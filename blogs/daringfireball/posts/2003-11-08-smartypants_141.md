[SmartyPants 1.4.1 is out.](https://daringfireball.net/projects/smartypants/) This is just a minor bug-fix release. The entirety of what’s new:

- The bug fix from 1.4 for dashes followed by quotes with no
intervening spaces now actually works.
- “`&nbsp;`” now counts as whitespace where necessary.
(Thanks to [Greg Knauss](http://eod.com/) for the patch.)
