After Bare Bones Software released BBEdit 7.0 last month, I planned to 
review the major new features. Procrastination eventually solves all 
problems, however — in this case by waiting for Michael Tsai’s 
comprehensive [review of BBEdit 
7.0 for the December issue of ATPM](http://atpm.com/8.12/bbedit.shtml). This is what every software review 
should be — detailed, researched, accurate, and most importantly, written 
by someone who has expertise with the application.
