When I announced the [new DF t-shirts](http://daringfireball.net/members/shirts) last month and my partnership with the fine folks at [Oddica](http://oddica.com/) for production and fulfillment, I had hoped the new shirts would start shipping by the end of April. I missed that mark, simply because it took me a few weeks longer than I had anticipated to figure out how many shirts we needed to print. (The new [Kevin Cornell-designed shirts](http://daringfireball.net/members/helmet) are a hit, to say the least.)


The good news is that the shirts are now in production, and they’ll start shipping worldwide by the end of next week.
