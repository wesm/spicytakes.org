Two brief quick notes to those of you who’ve recently joined or renewed as Daring Fireball members.

- If you’ve signed up but haven’t received your membership
information, please check your spam box. Several Gmail-using
members have found their membership confirmations there.
- If you’ve renewed a membership from last year, please note
that you must update your RSS subscription passwords to use your
new membership key as the password for  your feeds. In NetNewsWire,
select your Daring Fireball subscriptions, then use the Info
palette (Window → Info).
If you use Safari, you can update your subscription password using
the Keychain Access app (in the Utilities folder of your
Applications folder). Locate your “daringfireball.net” keychain
entries, and update the passwords to your new key value.
