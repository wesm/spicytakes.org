Amazon.com has [Mac OS X 10.4 Tiger available for pre-order](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/asin/B0002G71T0/ref=nosim/daringfirebal-20)
(sans release date), with a $35 rebate that brings the cost down to
just $95. That’s a pretty good deal, so I’ve placed a link to the
offer in this site’s banner. If you’re planning on upgrading to
Tiger (and I’m guessing somewhere around 90 percent of you are),
this is a good way to save yourself $35 and support Daring Fireball
at the same time.


(Alas, as of this writing, this offer is only available at
[Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/redirect?tag=daringfirebal-20&path=subst/home/home.html); neither [Amazon.ca](http://www.amazon.ca/exec/obidos/redirect-home?site=amazon&tag=daringfireb06-20) nor [Amazon.co.uk](http://www.amazon.co.uk/exec/obidos/redirect-home?tag=daringfirebal-21&site=amazon) have Tiger
available for pre-order yet.)
