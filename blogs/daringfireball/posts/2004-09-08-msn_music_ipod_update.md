The MSN Music help page regarding iPod support [discussed here last
week](http://daringfireball.net/2004/09/choose_microsoft) has been rewritten yet again, this time, [sans propaganda](http://beta.music.msn.com/help/ipod):


> The iPod does not currently support the Windows Media Audio
> (WMA) format and will not natively play any songs
> purchased from MSN Music, or any other Windows Media-based
> music service. If you are an iPod owner and would like to
> play MSN Music songs on your iPod, you can [send feedback
> to Apple](http://www.apple.com/feedback/ipod.html) and ask them to change their policy and add
> support for the Windows Media format.
